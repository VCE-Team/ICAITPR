Sponsorship Details

The ICAITPR-2024 aims to provide a forum for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to present and exchange their ideas in the latest technological advancements and innovations in the field of this fast-evolving domains of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Pattern Recognition. It is expected that more than 1000(one Thousand) delegates will participate in the event across the globe as this conference is an International repute.

Special events and tracks are being conducted for the industry as "Industry Connect". In this regard, the ICAITPR-2024 is requesting relevant industry to participate in the conference with their capabilities and showcase their product at the event.

Along with the "Industry Connect" industry can participate in the conference in many other forms like arrangement of Tutorials, Technical sessions related to the core and applications of industry, Exhibitions, Open forums, and plenary discussions, etc. by sponsoring the event directly.

All the industry will be provided with the standard participation as per the sponsorship packages listed below:

Standard Sponsorship Packages:

  • Recognition as a sponsor (with organization logo) on the sponsorship page of the official Conference website, including a hyperlink to the organizations home page.
  • Recognition as a sponsor (with organization logo) on the sponsorship page within the official conference proceedings.
  • Acknowledgment as a sponsor on the official sponsor acknowledgment signage situated onsite at the Conference venue.
  • Use of the Conference logo for marketing purposes until the end of December 2024.
  • Permission to place the stall near the Conference venue ICAITPR-2024.
  • The details package is listed in the table:

  • Organisation Logo on ICAITPR-2024 Delegate registration form.
