Sponsorship Details

The ICAITPR-2026 aims to provide a forum for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to present and exchange their ideas in the latest technological advancements and innovations in the field of this fast-evolving domains of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Pattern Recognition. It is expected that more than 1000(one Thousand) delegates will participate in the event across the globe as this conference is an International repute.

Financial Sponsor:

Vardhaman College of Engineering, Hyderabad, is the Financial Sponsor of ICAITPR-2026. A financial sponsor receives a portion of the surplus or is liable for a portion of the deficit resulting from the conference, and has direct and substantial involvement in the development of the technical program.

Technical Sponsor:

IEEE Hyderabad Section is the Technical Co-Sponsor of ICAITPR-2026. A technical sponsor has direct and substantial involvement in the development of the technical program, but holds no financial stake in the conference surplus or deficit.

Patron/Supporter:

Contributes to the conference financially or by hosting, but they do not assume responsibility for any loss incurred, nor do they receive any surplus. They are also not directly involved in the development of the technical program.

Special events and tracks are being conducted for the industry as "Industry Connect". In this regard, the ICAITPR-2026 is requesting relevant industry to participate in the conference with their capabilities and showcase their product at the event.

Along with the "Industry Connect" industry can participate in the conference in many other forms like arrangement of Tutorials, Technical sessions related to the core and applications of industry, Exhibitions, Open forums, and plenary discussions, etc. by sponsoring the event directly.

All the industry will be provided with the standard participation as per the support/patron packages listed below:

Standard Support/Patron Packages:

  • Recognition as a sponsor (with organization logo) on the sponsorship page of the official Conference website, including a hyperlink to the organizations home page.
  • Recognition as a sponsor (with organization logo) on the sponsorship page within the official conference proceedings.
  • Acknowledgment as a sponsor on the official sponsor acknowledgment signage situated onsite at the Conference venue.
  • Use of the Conference logo for marketing purposes until the end of December 2026.
  • Permission to place the stall near the Conference venue ICAITPR-2026.
